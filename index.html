Conference Objective

The primary objective of ICPASD-1 is to establish a premier platform for scientists, researchers, and industry leaders to exchange knowledge and pioneering discoveries in physics. In full alignment with Egypt Vision 2030, this conference seeks to bridge the gap between academic research and industrial application. By leveraging advanced scientific instrumentation and innovation, we aim to contribute to a knowledge-based economy and develop sustainable solutions that address critical national challenges in energy, health, and the environment.

Conference Venue

The conference will be held in Sharm El Sheikh, a world-renowned destination known as the City of Peace. Strategically located on the southern tip of the Sinai Peninsula along the Gulf of Aqaba, the city provides an inspiring backdrop for scientific exchange.

Bordered by the protected reserves of Naba and Ras Mohammed National Parks, Sharm El Sheikh offers a unique blend of natural beauty and modern infrastructure. Participants will benefit from the city's exceptional hospitality, world-class resorts, and a serene environment that fosters focus, networking, and collaboration.

Join us in this beautiful location to share knowledge, build connections, and contribute to sustainable development through physics.
